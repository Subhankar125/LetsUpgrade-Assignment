Knock-out format rules:

The top 8 players will play the Quarterfinals. Losers from the Quarterfinals will be given the rankings 5, 6, 7, and 8 in descending order of their opponentâ€™s standard Fide rating. For example: The player who loses to the highest rated player will be ranked 5th. The Quater finals and Semifinals will be played in 2 game match format with 3 min + 2-sec increment from move 1 with alternate colors. If the score gets tied then the tie will be resolved with the Armageddon game. Armageddon game will be played with 5 min to the White player and 4 minutes to the Black player with Black odds. (Draw is sufficient for the black player to advance in next stage/ to win the tournament where White needs to win in order to advance) There will not be any increment in an Armageddon match. The Finals will be played in 2 game match format with 5 min + 2-sec increment from move 1 with alternate colors. If the score gets tied then the tie will be resolved with the Armageddon game.
